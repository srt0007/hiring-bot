## How to Use

### Step 1: Launch Web UI

Double-click: `RUN_WEB_UI.bat`

Opens at: http://localhost:8501

### Step 2: Upload Resumes

1. Go to "Process Resumes" page
2. Drag & drop PDF or DOCX files
3. Click "Process Resumes"
4. View results!

### Step 3: Review Candidates

1. Go to "Review Candidates" page
2. Filter by fit score
3. Approve/reject candidates
